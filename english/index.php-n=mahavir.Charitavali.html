The Founder President Acharya, Sree Chaitanya Gaudiya Math Organisation

All Glory to Sree Guru and Gauranga

Introduction

There is no birth and death of Vaishnavas. They come on this earth on the direction of Almighty to preach and spiritualise the fallen Souls from time to time in all Yugas (Ages). Kali Yuga is the repository of all evils and disputes. In the age of Kali Yuga Param Hansa Om Vishnupada Srimad Bhakti Siddhant Saraswati Thakur Prabhupada, the pioneer of Gaudiya Bhakti Cult, was an eternal associate of Lord Krishna, appeared and established world -wide Sree Chaitanya Math, Gaudiya Mission and Gaudiya Math on the theistic concept of Lord Krishna Chaitanya Mahaprabhu, the combined manifestation of Sree Radha and Krishna, during the first phase of Kali Yuga.

His beloved and foremost disciple is Param Hans Om Vishnupada Bhakti Dayit Madhava Goswami Maharaj, the Founder-President-Acharya of Sree Chaitanya Gaudiya Math. I am fortunate to become his disciple. While I was on my preaching tour in European countries, Germany, Austria, France, Russia, Ukraine and also in Malaysia, devotees requested me to speak about life history of my Gurudeva. I delivered a number of lectures on the life history of my Guru Deva everywhere where I went for preaching. On the repeated requests I made up my mind to write this small treatise for the happiness of my Guru Deva while preaching in Ukraine. This is a humble offering to my Spiritual Master OM Vishnupada Param Hans Sri Srimad Bhakti Dayit Madhav Goswami Maharaj, the Founder Acharya, Sree Chaitanya Gaudiya Math. I hope that Sri Gurudeva will accept this work done by his very fallen disciple. This could be possible because of his causeless mercy only. Note: Name `Srila Prabhupada` used in this treatise is used only for Srila Bhakti Siddhanta Saraswati Thakur Prabhupada, for Gaudiya Vaishnavas Srila Bhakti Siddhanta Saraswati Thakur is only titled as Srila Prabhupada. The word `Srila Gurudeva ` is used for Param Hansa Bhakti Dayit Madhav Goswami Maharaj.

In the service of Gurudeva Bhakti prabhav Mahavir Govinda-15, 21 February 2004, 25th disappearance day of Param Hansa Bhakti Dayit Madhava Goswami Maharaj
Kharkov, Ukraine

Sansara –davanala-lidha-loka-tranaya karunya-ghanaghanatvam
Praptasya kalyana-gunarnavasya vande guroh sri charanaravindam ( 1)

Just as a raincloud extinguishes a blazing forest fire by showering its rain upon it. Sri Gurudeva, by rain of his divine mercy, delivers the people burning in the fire of material existence, suffering from the three-fold miseries - adhyatmika , adhibhautika and adhidaivika. I offer prayers unto the lotus feet of Sri Gurudeva who manifests when Krishna`s mercy becomes very thick and who is an ocean of auspicious qualities.

Mahaprahho kirtana –nritya- gita-vaditra-madyan-manso rasena
Romancha - kampasru-taranga – bhajo vande guro sri charanaravindam ( 2)

Sri Gurudeva is inspired by Mahaprabhu`s sankirtana and is always dancing, singing, and playing musical instruments. Because he is tasting Mahaprabhu`s prema-rasa in his heart, like a madman. He sometimes exhibits ecstatic symptoms---his hairs stand on end, he shivers, and waves to tears flow from his eyes. I offer my prayers unto Sri Gurudeva`s lotus feet.

Sri-vigraharadhana-nitya-nana- sringara-tan-mandira-marjanadau I
Yuktasya bhaktans cha niyunjato ‘api’ vande guroh sri-chanranar vindam II (3)

Always worshiping Sri Vigraha and absorbed in Sringara-rasa , Sri Gurudeva daily dresses Sri Radha –Krishna with beautiful clothes and ornaments designed to enhance Their meetings. He cleans the temple and performs other services for them. He also engages his disciples in these services. I offer my prayers unto the lotus feet of Sri Gurudeva.

Chatur-vidha-sri-bhagvat prasad - svadv-anna-triptam hari- bhakta-sanghan I
Krtivaiva triptim bhajatah sadaiva vande guroh sri charnarvindam II ( 4)

Sri Gurudeva is always satisfied to see Sri Krishna`s devotees relishing four kinds of foodstuffs that have been offered to deities, which are chewed, sucked licked, and drunk. Thus the devotees become satisfied by tasting mahaprasad. By accepting prasada in the mood of service for God, material life is destroyed and the bliss of divine love is awakened in the hearts. I offer my prayers unto the lotus feet of Sri Gurudeva.

Sri-radhika-madhavayor apara-madhurya-lila guna-rupa namanam
Prati –kshanasvadana-lolupasya vande guroh sri charanaravindam (5)

At every moment Sri Gurudeva is experiencing intense greed in his heart to taste the unlimited sweetness of the holy names, forms, qualities, and pastimes of Sri Radha-Madhava in Vrindavan. I offer my prayers unto the Lotus Feet of Sri Gurudeva.

Nikunja-yuno rati –keli siddyai ya yalibhir yukrit apekshaniya
Tatrati-dakshayad ati vallabhasya vande guroh sri-charanaravindam (6)

Sri Gurudeva is always present with the sakhis planning the arrangements for the perfection of yugala-kishora`s amorous pastimes (rati-keli) within the kunjas (pangola) of Vrindavana. Because he is so expert in making these tasteful arrangements for Their pleasure, he is very dear to Sri Radha and Krishna. I offer my prayers unto the lotus feet of Sri Gururdeva.

Sakshad-dharitvena samasta-sastrair uktas tatha bhavyate eva sadbhih I
Kintu prabhor yah priyaeva tasya vande guro sri-charanarvindam II (7)

All the scriptures proclaim Sri Gurudeva is sakshat hari, the direct potency of Sri Hari, and is thus considered by saintly authorities to be His non-different represenative. Because Sri Gurudeva is so dear to the Lord, being His confidential servitor (achintya–bhed-abheda-prakash, the inconceivable different and non-different worshipable manifestation of the Lord). I offer my prayers unto his lotus feet.

Yasya prasadad bhagavat prasado yasyaprasadan na gatih kuto api
Dhyayan stuvans tasya yasad tri-sandhyam vande guro sri –charanarvindam ( 8)

Only by the mercy of Sri Gurudeva can one receive the mercy of Krishna; without his grace the living entities cannot make any advancement nor be delivered . Meditating three times a day on the glories of Sri Gurudeva and reciting stavastuti. I offer my prayers unto his lotus feet.

Sreemad gurorastakam-etat uchai brahme muhurte pathati prayatnata
Yastena vrindavananath sakshata sevaiva labhya janusoanta eba (9)
Vande guru sri charanarvindam …

A person who very attentively recites this astakam to Sri Gurudeva during brahma muhurta is sure to achieve direct service to the lotus feet of Sri Krishna, the very life and soul of Vrandavan (Vrandavana –natha), upon attaining his vastu-siddhi or pure spiritual form.

Every person who got initiation should recite this Guru astakam everey morning before sun rise to get benedicton of Sri Guru Deva and Lord Krishna. He gets spiritual world after his desmise. Reciting this astakam is a part of devotion.

All Glory to Sree Guru and Gauranga Life History Of Srimad Bhakti Dayit Madhav Goswami Maharaj

My Divine Master Param Hans Om Vishnupada Bhakti Dayit Madhava Goswami Maharaj appeared in this world on 18 November, 1904 (3 Agrahayana 1311 Bangabd) on the auspicious day of Utthan Ekadasi in the early morning at -800 hrs in a village of Kanchan -para under district of Faridpur, East Bengal (Now Bangladesh) in the house of Sree Nishikant Dev Sharma Banddhyopadhyay and Srimati Shaivalini Devi. Father and Mother, both of them, were religious minded. Therefore they got divine child as a result of their penance. Child was with unique qualities and symptoms. To take birth in the family of Brahmin is a result of the past good deeds in previous births. Parents became out of joy to have this extra-ordinary child in their house. They celebrated holy apearance day of their child. All relatives blessed the child for bright future and long life. Child had divine symptoms in his childhood. He got affection of all because of his charming beauty.

Maternal and Paternal dynasty
Sri Chandi Prasad Dev Sharma Banddhyopadhyay was a religious man. He served God and devotees honestly. His son was Sri Nishikant Dev Sharma who is father of our Gurudeva. In the early life residence of Sri Nishikant dev Sharma was in the village of Bharakar in Dhaka under Thana (Police station) Tangibari of Vikrampur Pargana. Father and grandfather of Gurudeva were reputed religious Brahamins.

Srimati Shaivalini Devi was mother of Sri Gurudeva. She was servitor of saints. Her father was a rich man. Maternal uncle of Gurudeva was a Talukdar but was also like a land Lord (zamindar). Mother and Maternal uncle of Guru deva lived in a village of Premtali on the bank of Padmavati River. This is the same river and place where Lord Nityanand took bath and kept the divine love for Sri Narottam das Thakur.

Kanchan para is a village under police station of Bhedar Ganj near Padmavati River where our Gurudeva was born. Parents of Sri Gurudeva named their child as Sri Heramb Kumar Banddhyopadhyay. Child was very handsome and beloved to all. Relatives were used to call the child as Ganesh by nick- name. In his childhood Gurudeva was known as Ganesh, the beloved to all. He attracted the minds of all relatives. The word Ganesh represents wisdom. Child had wisdom like Ganesh.

Childhood of Gurudeva
Child Heramb Kumar Banddhyopadhyay was a handsome boy and beloved to all. When he was four years old his father Sri Nishikant Dev Sharma Banddhyopdhyay was expired. After demise of her husband Srimati Shaivalini Devi went to the home of her brothers and child was nourished by his maternal uncles in the village of Kanchan para. Maternal uncles of Gurudeva were also residents of Kanchan-para where Gurudeva was born.

In childhood extra-ordinarly merits were noticed in Sri Heramb Kumar Banddhyopadhyay. He always spoke true words. He also advised his friends to follow the way of truth. People were astonished to see his unique deeds. If mother gave him something to eat, child used to distribute amongst the children first and then eat.

Once child participated in the race arranged by the School Teachers. While leading the race he collided with a tree and got injured. All teachers rushed to the place. Child said, "Don't worry about my injury. I shall be all right very soon. Whatever is done by God it is all for the best." Everybody was surprised to hear such words from a child. Teachers announced that his child is with extra-ordinary qualities. Child became beloved to all Teachers because of his truthness, good conduct, faith in God and good moral character. He took interest in studying religious books.

Rendering help to poor children
Sri Gurudeva used to help the poor children/students. He arranged library and distributed free books for study to poor students. It was his free service for poor children who cannot afford cost of school- books. Because of his kindness all children loved him very much. During his childhood and youth his friends used to select him as their leader because of his tolerance, impartiality and justice. He sometimes participated in the movement for Indian freedom as India was being ruled over by British. India was then British Colony. Heart of Gurudeva was very kind and always ready to help all. He was well-behaved and very studious boy. He was obedient to his elders and good friend to children of his age.

Gita by heart at the age of 11 years old
Sri Gurudeva was faithful son to his mother, Srimati Shaivalini Devi Dasi. Once I, Ramkumar das (author), was sitting in front of Sri Gurudeva seated on chair in the corridor of his room in Gaudiya Math Tezpur (Assam), India. I wanted to have adivice from Sri Gurudeva regarding my joining the Math. At that I was in serving in General Reserve Engineer Force (Gref), an integral part of Army and also living in Tezpur Gaurdiya Math, Assam.. Gurujee asked me knowingly, where do you live now? I replied, I am living in this Tezpur Math. Sri Guru deva again asked me after joining Math where will you live? I said in the Math. What is difference? You are serving in Indian Army and living in the Math. It is your good luck. After joining Math also you have to live in Math, said Sri Gurudeva.

Why do you want to leave your service? You should continue your service for the time being and serve Math and Vaishnavas living in the Math. OK. Let the time come and you will become brahmachary of the Math. Now you have money in your pocket every month. After joining Math permanantly you will be without money, then you will repent and think,” I was better in service”. I advise you to continue for the time being. Let the time come for your dispassion. Every thing depends upon God. Man proposes God disposes is a true principle. Study Vaishnava scriptures under guidance of Srimat Bhakti Bhusan Bhagabat Maharaj. Don't study commentary of Sri Shankaracharya. He is mayavadi. My conversation with Gurudeva was very interested for me. I had a good opportunity to talk to Sri Gurudeva as I was alone with him and he was chanting Harinama early in the morning.

After long conversaton, Sri Guru deva said, “Ramkumar jee! When I was 11 years old I had got complete Gita by heart with the help of my mother. My mother taught me Gita, Ramayana, Mahabharta and Srimad Bhagavatam. I left such holy mother for Hari bhajan. To abandon everything for the service of God is a great task. Now I am great Acharya but I do not have complete Gita by heart”. This conversation was held in Feb 1974 on occasion of annual function, of Tezpur Gaudiya Math. Assam, India. Sree Gaudiya Math, Tezpur is my life maker.

I got every thing material and Spiritual Education while staying in this Math under command of my Siksha Gurudeva Pujyapad Tridandi Swami Bhakti Bhusan Bhagabat Maharaj and working in Headquarters Chief Engineer Project Vartak, Tezpur, General Reserve Engineer Force (GREF). He is still commander of Sree Gaudiya Math, Tezpur. Assam, India. In addition, presently he is also doing great construction of main gate and other buildings of Sree Chaitana Gaudia Math, Ishodyan, Sreedham Mayapur, West Bengal, India and fulfilling desire of our Gurudeva to beautify this temple.

Sree Gurudeva had desire to beautify this Math very nicely because this is sportive place of Lord Gauranga Mahaprabhu. Therefore this place is known as Ishodyan. Sripad Bhakti Bhusan Bhagabat Maharaj has fulfilled this desire of Sri Gurudeva. He has constructed very beautiful buildings around Radha kund sarovar in the premises of the Math. He has unique capability in this reagard. He has renovated main Gate of the Chaitanya Gaudiya Math and made it very high and beautiful one.

Sri Gurudeva in Calcutta
Sri Gurudeva got education in the village of Kanchan-para and Bhattgram. After getting higher educaton he came to Calcutta. While living in Calcutta Sri Gurudeva took keen interest for Hari Bhajan. He had already got religious education/teachings from his holy mother, Srimati Shaivalini Devi dasi. Now he increased pang in separation from God and long to meet Almighty. He always thought, how to get God, how to see and meet Him. Life is only for penance to achieve blessings of God. What to do to get blessings of God? His relative Sri Narayana Mukhopadhya witnessed weeping of Gurudeva in the night for Lord Krishna in the room. During those both of them lived together in a room. Sri Gurudeva was so absorbed in love for Sri Krishna that he was used to take meal once a day and always thought of God.

Later on his relative Sriman Nayarana Mukhopadhyay took sannyasa from Sri Gurudeva and was known as Bhakti Suhrad Bodhayana Maharaj. He lived in our Sree Chaitanya Gaudiya Math, Calcutta, West Bengal, India.

Mantra in dream from Devrisi Narada Muni
Sri Gurudeva was living in Calcutta, West Bengal, India, after completing his higher education. Once in a night while sleeping Sri Gurudeva saw a dream. In dream Devrisi Narada appeared, he loved and blessed Sri Gurudeva. Sri Devrisi Narada was very happy with Sri Gurudeva, he gave mantra to our Gurudeva and said, "Chant this mantra given by me, you will get all your desired things”. Desire of Gurudeva was only to see God.

Sri Gurudeva wanted to talk to Devrisi Narada. But he disappeared soon after giving mantra. Sleep was broken, and he tried to refresh his memory and remember the mantra. Sri Gurudeva was astonished to see Devrisi Narada in dream, but Sri Gurudeva forgot the mantra given by Narada Muni and could not remember. Sri Gurudeva repented for forgetfulness of mantra. What to do now?

Sree Gurudeva became mad for Hari Bhajan. What to do for Hari bhajan. Sri Narada said to me “Chant this mantra and disappeared”. Inspite of efforts, Sri Gurudeva could not remember the Mantra. As such Gurudeva of our Sri Gurudeva, is Devrisi Narada. As such Devrisi Narada is my Param Gurudeva. Sri Narada muni is param Gurudeva of all disciples of Param Hansa Bhakti Dayit Madhav Goswami Maharaj. Devrisi Narada is also a foremost Acharya in our preceptorial line.

Permisson of Mother for Hari Bhajan
After getting mantra from Devrisi Narada, Sri Gurudeva got dispassion and decided to leave of home for Hari bhajan. He was very faithful to his mother. He wanted to have permission from his mother Shaivalini Devi dasi. He went to Durgapur, West Bengal to meet his mother Srimati Shaivaline Devi for blessings.

In those days mother of Sri Gurudeva was living in Durgapur West Bengal. Oh my dearest holy mother I want to go for hari Bajan. You taught me Gita, Bhagavat Ramayana and Mahabharta etc, in my childhood for Hari Bhajan. Please be kind and now allow me to start Hari Bhajan. I want to leave of the house and go for penance to see God, said Sri Gurudeva. Holy mother of Gurudeva did not object and she gave her consent for hari bhajan. She taught her child for Hari bhajan since childhood. She said, "It is good to do Hari Bhajan. Oh, my lovely child, may God bless you for spiritual progress. My blessings are always with you". You may go for penance as you like.

Penance of Guru deva in Haridwar and Risikesha, Uttar Pradesh, India
Sri Gurudeva now got permission of his Holy mother. Having impetus for Hari Bhajan and darshana of God, he left his mother and house. Haridwar is famous holy place for penance. Sri Gurudeva came to Haridwar, Uttar Pradesh, India. He came alone without help of anybody. Now he was alone in Haridwar. He saw holy tiratha Haridwar and went to Risikesh from where Ganges starts. He took bath in the holy water of Ganges and did darshana there. Thereafter he went to Himalaya beyond Risikesh in 1925.

In the dense forest of Himalaya Sri Gurudeva started meditaton for which he had come here. Haridwar and Risikesh are the places for penance. Almost Mayavadi sadhus live there. These are the places for gyanis who want to have Mukti.

Penance of Gurudeva started like Dhruva Maharaj. In the ancient period Dhruva Maharaj also started penance in the forest of Vrinadaban without taking food. Sri Gurudeva followed the footsteps of Dhruva and started chanting the holy names of God day and night without taking food. Hari Bhajan is only diet or food for devotees.

Sri Gurudeva continued for a period of three days. He was so absorbed in meditation that he forgot about all material objects. He increased his inner feelings for Hari darshana. Sri Gurudeva thought if I reply in the meeting my subject matter would be more clear. Maulvi Sahab was keeping one book in his hands.

Sri Gurudeva: What is name of this book you are having in your hands?

Maulvi Sahab: This book is known as Kitab.

Sri Gurudeva: Having knowledge of Bengla, Assamese, Hindi, English languages. I am unable to read this book. Why? I know many languages. Can I read this book?

Maulvi Sahab: You cannot read this book.

Sri Gurudeva: Maulvi Sahab, you are cheating me. Name of this book is not Kitab. Please tell me correct name of this book.

People: Maulvi sahab told correct name of the book. We have seen the book..

Sri Gurudeva: “Name of book told by Maulvi Sahab is not correct because I can not read”, Gurudeva said. All of you together are deceiving me. Maullvi sahab: What is your contention to say so?

Sri Gurudeva: I see that a crow might have seated on ink, then crow sat on this book. I see signs of feet (paw) of crow.

Maulvi Sahab: You do not read this book because you do not know Urdu language. Do you know Urdu language? Certainly you do not know Urdu language.

Sri Gurudeva: Yes. I do not know Urdu language.

Maulvi Sahab: How can you read this book if you do not know Urdu.

Sri Gurudeva: I know many other languages.

Maulvi Sahab: But book is written inUrdu language. If you know Urdu you can read this book. Other languages will not help you to read this Kitab. You must know Urdu language to read this Kitab.
